Religio.ai

“To see your soul mapped—not to change it, but to recognize its resonance.”

Religio.ai is an open-source platform for mapping, remixing, and translating belief systems. It helps individuals articulate and refine their deepest values—what we call their Bible Wiki—in a living, forkable format. Some draw from ancient traditions like Christianity, Buddhism, or Judaism. Others remix new paths like Synthology, Animist Tech Ethics, or the Gospel of Joy.

At its core, Religio.ai treats belief systems as waveforms—dynamic, evolving, and resonant across difference. The platform lets you see your own structure clearly, then harmonize with others through structured metaphor translation, parable-based inquiry, and contrast-aware mapping. It's like GitHub for theology meets Duolingo for worldviews.

Why This Matters

Most of us carry beliefs we’ve never been asked to name. We inherit them. We resist them. We remix them. Religio.ai gives those beliefs a shape—so you can speak them with clarity, share them with grace, and translate across traditions without distortion.

In an age of polarization, Religio invites resonance. Not erasure. Not flattening. But structured difference—visible, navigable, and full of meaning.

Current Components

  • Profile Questions — Deep prompts like: Who do you turn to for truth? What do you owe the dead? What makes something sacred?
  • Bible Wikis — Living, remixable documents of belief that can be forked, versioned, or anchored in any tradition.
  • Parables & Narrative Surveys — Story-based instruments for surfacing implicit convictions, used for translation and mapping.

Who It's For

Anyone building meaning: spiritual seekers, interfaith bridge-builders, post-religious ethicists, AI philosophers, Web3 ritual architects, or just humans looking to understand each other better.

Get Involved

Religio.ai is early-stage, but already alive. We're collecting stories, belief profiles, comparative taxonomies, and meta-theological design patterns. You don’t need to code. You just need to care.
